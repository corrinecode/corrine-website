================================
IMAGES FOLDER
================================

Put all your images here!

NAMING TIPS:
- Use lowercase letters
- Use dashes instead of spaces
- Examples: my-photo.jpg, article-cover.png, profile-pic.jpg

RECOMMENDED SIZES:
- Article/post featured images: 1200-1600px wide
- Profile photo: 500-800px wide
- In-content images: 800-1200px wide

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- .jpg / .jpeg (best for photos)
- .png (best for graphics with text)
- .gif (for simple animations)
- .webp (modern format, smaller files)

HOW TO USE IN YOUR CONTENT:
- Featured image: "image": "images/your-file.jpg"
- In body text: [IMAGE: images/your-file.jpg]
- With caption: [IMAGE: images/your-file.jpg]\n[CAPTION: Your caption here.]
