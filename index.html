I'm a writer, editor, and producer.

I am the co-founder of Club Chess, a party that has been featured in The New York Times, New York Magazine, and the New York Post. More on Club Chess can be heard in my talk 3D Chess: The Metaphysics of Party Culture.

I was the editorial producer for Zora Zine, a magazine that looked toward the future of technology and the cultural discourse surrounding it. I was also Zora's head of editorial and events, shaping how the company spoke about itself and all the ways it faced the public.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, and find the Hollywood sign reassuring. I now live in New York City, and feel the same way about the Empire State Building.

I am currently freelancing on all things copy, editing, and events.

I am most interested in seeing what I can get away with.
