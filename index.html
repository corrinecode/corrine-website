I'm a writer, editor, and producer.

I am the co-founder of Club Chess. A party that can best be described by New York Magazine, The New York Times, and Crack Magazine.

I was the editorial producer for Zora Zine, a magazine that looked towards the future of technology and cultural discourse that surrounds it. I am most proud of the print book I worked on, published by Actual Source.

I have given a talk on The Metaphysics of Party Culture.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, and find the Hollywood sign reassuring. I now live in New York City, and feel the same way about the Empire State Building.

I am currently the writer and head of events at Zora.

I am most interested in seeing what I can get away with.
