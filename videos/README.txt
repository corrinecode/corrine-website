================================
VIDEOS FOLDER
================================

Put all your videos here!

NAMING TIPS:
- Use lowercase letters
- Use dashes instead of spaces
- Examples: my-video.mp4, behind-the-scenes.mp4

RECOMMENDED FORMAT:
- MP4 with H.264 codec (works in all browsers)
- Keep file sizes under 50MB if possible
- Compress large videos before adding

HOW TO USE IN YOUR CONTENT:
- In body text: [VIDEO: videos/your-video.mp4]

The video will automatically have play/pause controls.
